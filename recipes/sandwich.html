Sandwich

Description

A budget-friendly sandwich recipe that's extremely delicious and a perfect breakfast/lunch/dinner for college students!

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Turkey Slices
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Mayonnaise

Steps:

  1. Spread mayonnaise on two slices of bread.
  2. Put some lettuce on the lower bread slice, followed by 1-2 slices of cheese.
  3. Add the tomatoes next.
  4. Next, add the turkey slices and some seasoning if you like.
  5. Finish the sandwich by putting the second bread slice on top!
